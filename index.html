Frequently Asked Questions

This hackathon hosted by CSU East Bay and our local community colleges is intended to encourage students at all skill levels to collaborate and get project experience. Whether you're a freshman with no experience or a senior with a few projects under your belt, this event is for you!


There will also be several workshops over the weekend designed to help you not just with the hackathon projects but to help make you better programmers overall.

For Cal State East Bay students: Priority will be given to MESA U members, then any Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or Data Science majors. If you would like to join MESA U, apply here.

For local community college students: Any student, but with priority given to MESA students

No, competitors cannot work on their project prior to the beginning of the event although brainstorming potential ideas is allowed.

Each team can have up to 4 people.

No, you can compete solo if you'd like but there will be opening activities to aid in the team creation process.

Yes, there will be plenty of food and snacks throughout the event provided by CSU East Bay's dining commons! Refer to the schedule for breakfast and lunch times.

A laptop or device capable of coding is essential. We also recommend bringing:

  • Laptop charger
  • Any cables or adapters you might need
  • A water bottle to stay hydrated
